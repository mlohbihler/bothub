Challenge: Turn

In this challenge you need to move forward and turn. This time the target is not right in front of the agent. There are two ways to accomplish this...

You could turn the agent until it is pointing at the target, and then move. The trick here is that you need to store state, i.e. turning vs moving.

Or, you can turn and move at the same time in the right proportions to hit the target. The trouble is finding values that work.

The sample code provides almost-working versions of each approach.

TIP: If you can't see the agent or goal area, you can scale the environment view with your mouse wheel, or drag it around.

Sensors

This challenge doesn't have any sensors. Focus your attention on the actuators for now.

Actuators

Key Description Range
speed How fast the agent moves forward or backward. -0.5 ... 4
turn Rate of turning. -π/12 ... π/12
sidle How fast the agent moves side to side. -0.4 ... 0.4

Goal

Move the agent into the goal area, being the yellow circle. It will turn green when the goal is achieved. You cannot press any keys during execution, otherwise the goal area will deactivate and turn grey/gray tomato/tomato.
